HUMEXR

1. Gazette Notification number and Date: S.O. 2346 (E) Dated 26-05-2025

2. Title of Bio Stimulant: Humates (12.5%) (Liquid)

3. Composition:

  • Humic Acid per cent by weight, minimum: 10.0%
  • Fulvic Acid per cent by weight, minimum: 2.5%
  • pH (10% aqueous solution): 12 - 14
  • Specific Gravity: 1.0 - 1.20

4. Crops: Green Gram

5. Dosage: Two soil applications at 5L/ha.
